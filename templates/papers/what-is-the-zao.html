01 - What is The ZAO?

The ZAO moves profit, data, and ownership back to creators, powered by blockchain and AI. Music is the proving ground for this model - the first artist domain, but not the last.

Priorities in order: music first, community second, technology third. Blockchain and AI are enablers, not the story.

02 - What does ZAO stand for?

ZTalent Artist Organization

The acronym unpacked. Today, call it a decentralized artist network - the acronym names it, this framing describes what it does.

03 - Who founded The ZAO?

Zaal Panthaki (BetterCallZaal)

Zaal Panthaki (BetterCallZaal) founded and leads The ZAO. He currently funds most operations while the network builds toward self-sustaining revenue through tools, events, grants, and partnerships[1].

04 - Is The ZAO a record label?

No - not a label, not just a music community

The ZAO is neither a label nor just a music community - it's a network built on returned ownership. Labels seize control of an artist's work; The ZAO returns profit margin, data, and IP rights instead. Technology is the mechanism, not the message.

05 - What does The ZAO return to artists?

Profit margin, data, and IP rights

Ownership shifts from middlemen to artists. Blockchain and AI handle the logistics, not the pitch. WaveWarZ is the clearest running proof: artists are paid instantly, and the large majority of every dollar traded stays in the ecosystem instead of leaking to a middleman[2].

06 - How does governance work?

The Fractal and Respect

The Fractal is the governance engine - a weekly Respect Game ranks contributions, OREC (optimistic execution with commitment-and-reveal) processes the outcomes, and a Fibonacci curve distributes the rewards. Respect is the soulbound on-chain contribution token (an OG ERC-20 plus a ZOR ERC-1155, both on Optimism)[3].

07 - What is Respect?

The on-chain contribution currency

A soulbound reputation token (an OG ERC-20 plus a ZOR ERC-1155) on Optimism. Earned by contributing, ranked weekly in the Respect Game, shaped by a Fibonacci reward curve. Because it is soulbound, it cannot be bought, sold, or transferred - only earned[3].

08 - What is the Fractal?

The governance process

Weekly Respect Game ranks contributions, OREC settles the outcomes, Fibonacci curve shapes the rewards. This is how artists and builders coordinate without a traditional top-down label structure - contribution over capital, not money buying influence[1].

09 - What does The ZAO build?

Four production lanes

WaveWarZ

Live music battles traded like prediction markets - the entry point for most new participants.

ZABAL Games

A three-month intensive for builders: workshops, shipping sprints, real products.

The ZAO festivals

Live events under a festivals umbrella - ZAOstock, ZAOville, ZAO-PALOOZA, ZAO-CHELLA.

ZAO OS

The lab and monorepo where new ZAO things incubate before they graduate to their own homes.

Several other brands live inside the ecosystem beyond these four flagship lanes (SongJam, FISHBOWLZ, the ZABAL token, ZOUNZ governance, and more) - see the full papers index for their individual drafts.

10 - What is WaveWarZ?

Live-traded music battles

One of The ZAO's production lanes: music competes in a prediction-market format, artists are paid instantly, and value stays in the ecosystem rather than leaking to a middleman[2].

11 - What is ZABAL Games?

A three-month build-a-thon

Builders work through workshops and sprints to ship real products in the ecosystem - the bar is one small, shippable move, not a big build.

12 - What are the ZAO festivals?

ZAOstock, ZAOville, ZAO-PALOOZA, ZAO-CHELLA

The ecosystem's live events, run under a festivals umbrella - real-world gatherings that turn online community into real-world networks.

13 - How do I become a member?

Sign the Manifesto

Sign the Manifesto - a short creed of five commitments - and it becomes an on-chain hat (via Hats Protocol, ERC-1155, soulbound and non-transferable, on Optimism)[6]. Read the full creed at thezao.xyz/papers/manifesto.

14 - What blockchain does The ZAO use?

Optimism, plus Base and Solana by lane

Respect governance tokens (the soulbound OG ERC-20 and the ZOR ERC-1155) live on Optimism. The ecosystem spans other chains by need - WaveWarZ, for example, runs on Solana and Base.
