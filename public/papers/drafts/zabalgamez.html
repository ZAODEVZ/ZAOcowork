ZABAL Games - Whitepaper (draft v0.1)

ZABAL Games

The ZAO's three-month build-a-thon. Ship something real in public, with a ZAO mentor in your corner, and earn Respect plus an on-chain credential. Build for a real community, not a weekend you forget.

What it is

ZABAL Games is a free, Farcaster-native onboarding event - anyone is welcome. It runs three tracks so anyone can step in: artist (musical and visual builds), builder (developer and aspiring-coder builds), and creator (media, distribution, and content builds). The audience that matters is the 100-plus member ZAO community, so what you make lands in front of real people, not a leaderboard you forget.

The arc

June is workshops - ZAO teachers and ecosystem partners cover governance, Respect, and the tools (Claude Code, Empire Builder, POIDH, Juke, SongJam), watchable live or async at zabalgamez.com/recordings.

July is the open build month. The goal set for 2026 is 200 distinct builders, multiple submissions each. Ship a live URL, an open-source repo, a short demo, and a cast to /zabal. Every build that clears the bar earns Respect - partial and work-in-progress drafts count.

August is the Finals. Mentor-and-builder pairs lock, then a 24-hour build, a promote window, and a Respect-weighted vote with a live reveal stream. Finalists are paid in USDC on a tiered scale.

How it works

Enter on the site (/enter) with a wallet and a GitHub repo; signups and collectibles run through Magnetiq. Mentors are an open roster, not a fixed panel - each mentor defines the builder archetype they want and pairs with an open-submission builder, and they receive a Hats Protocol ZAO Mentor role. Submissions live on a public GitHub-backed board, with Bonfire (the knowledge graph) as the system of record. Empire Builder and Clanker are optional tools for leaderboards and token launches, not requirements.

What you walk away with

A live artifact and an open repo. July finishers earn Respect, the ZAO's soulbound contribution currency. All finalists receive a Hats Protocol collectible NFT on Base - a universal "I shipped at ZABAL Games" credential.

Role in The ZAO

ZABAL Games is the front door for builders, the parallel to WaveWarZ (the front door for fans and players). Both feed the same community: people arrive, make something real, and become contributors with Respect to show for it.

Find it

zabalgamez.com (with a Z; the old zabalgames.com redirects here). Signup and collectibles at app.magnetiq.xyz under the ZABAL brand. On Farcaster: the /zabal channel; tag posts "zabal gamez". A stream overlay for workshops and finals is at zaoos.com/overlay/zabal-games.
