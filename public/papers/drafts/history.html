History

A dated index of what The ZAO has actually shipped - events, products, partnerships - each entry linked to where you can read more. Not a story yet. A table of contents for one.

This is the paper version: a plain, linkable timeline. The real ask - a smooth, collaborative UI where anyone in the community can submit their own entry to the timeline - is a separate, bigger build. This page is the scaffold for that: get the dates and links right first, then build the interface on top.

The ZAO's founding

The exact founding date is not yet confirmed in the research library. The earliest dated, sourced entry below is April 2024 (ZAO-PALOOZA). If The ZAO's community and governance predate that - which they likely do - that history is not yet written down anywhere this index can cite. Open gap, not an invented date.
