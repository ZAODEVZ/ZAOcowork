The ZAO - Team Profile

Django (JANGOUU)

Musician, origin story figure. Working with him shifted Zaal toward thinking entrepreneurially about how to support independent artists. The seed of The ZAO.

The Origin Story

Zaal met Django in college, 2018 or 2019. Collaborating with him crystallized something Zaal had been thinking about: how to support independent artists not by controlling their work, but by giving them tools and community to own it themselves. That idea became The ZAO.

Identity

Music

DIY producer, engineer, and songwriter. He writes, produces, engineers, and mixes his own work across a genre blend of synthrock, trap, folk-pop, and ambient R&B. Also produces music NFTs.

Latest release: TSRCV2 EP, December 31, 2025 (5 tracks, including "ASHES 2 ASHES," "FUCK IT BET," "BEFORE I BLITZ // HIGH OFF A FEELIN'")

Earlier work: THE SYNTHROCK CONCEPT V1 (2024), SHATTERPOINT and WHEN I'M HIGH (singles, 2025), feature on "Stuck" from Even Summer Ends (2024)
